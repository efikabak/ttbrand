Foundation · Logo

Safe Zone

To protect the logo from other visual elements, it must be surrounded by a minimum amount of clear space at all times.

Minimum Clear Space
½ X ½ X ½ X ½ X

The safe zone on all four sides equals ½ X, where X is the full width of the symbol icon. No typography, imagery, or graphic elements may enter this space.

Foundation · Logo

Logo Don'ts

Our mark has been built with precision. Any distortion, alteration, or misuse weakens what it stands for.

Incorrect Usage
Do not distort

Never stretch, squash, or alter proportions.

Do not add effects

No drop shadows, glows, outlines, or gradients.

Do not alter colors

Only use approved color variants. Never recolor arbitrarily.

Do not use on patterns

Always place on a clean, solid-color background.

Do not resize the icon

The icon and wordmark must stay in fixed proportion.

Do not use on busy imagery

Ensure sufficient contrast and clear space at all times.
