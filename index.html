Primary Logo

The primary logo consists of the T&T symbol and the Treasure Trove wordmark. Use the variant that provides the best visibility for the given background.

Primary Logo — Dark

Default. For dark or black backgrounds

Primary Logo — Light

For light or white backgrounds

Gold Treatment

Use only if necessary — reserved contexts

Symbol

The T&T mark used independently when the brand name is already established in context — app icons, favicons, social avatars, compact placements.

Symbol — Dark

App icon, favicon, social avatar on dark

Symbol — Light

App icon, favicon, social avatar on light

Stacked Logo

The stacked lockup places the wordmark below the symbol. Use for square-format and vertical layout contexts.

Stacked — Dark

On dark or black backgrounds

Stacked — Light

Incorrect Usage

Avoid these misuses to maintain the integrity of the Treasure Trove brand.

Don't
Don't distort or skew

Never stretch, squash, or skew.

Don't
Don't use unapproved colors

White, Black, or Yellow only.

Don't
Don't add effects

No shadows, glows, or outlines.
