Safe Zone

To protect the logo from other visual elements, it must be surrounded by a minimum amount of clear space at all times. No other graphic elements should encroach within this area. The size of this safe zone is defined by the half-width of the icon.

X½ safe zone

Logo Don'ts

The mark has been built with precision. Any distortion, alteration, or misuse weakens what it stands for. These are not stylistic preferences — they are the rules that protect the integrity of the brand.

Do not distort

Never stretch, squash, or alter proportions in any way.

Do not add effects

No shadows, glows, outlines, or filters. Keep it clean.

Do not alter colors

Only use the three approved color treatments.

Do not add patterns

Never place the logo over patterns or busy backgrounds.

Do not resize the icon

Keep icon and wordmark proportions locked.

Do not use on crowded images

Ensure sufficient contrast and clear background.
