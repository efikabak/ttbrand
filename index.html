Logo Dont's

Our mark has been built with precision. Any distortion, alteration, or misuse weakens what it stands for. These are not stylistic preferences — they are the rules that protect the integrity of the brand.

Don't
Do not distort the logo

Never stretch, squash, skew or alter proportions.

Don't
Do not add effects

No shadows, glows, outlines or filters.

Don't
Do not alter the colors

Use only approved color variants.

Don't
Do not add pattern behind logo

Always use a clean, solid background.

Don't
Do not change the size of the icon

The icon and wordmark must stay proportional.

Don't
Do not use on crowded images

Ensure sufficient clear space and contrast.
